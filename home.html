Eden is a stage name for the performer Jonathan Ng. Eden is Irish and has played many musical instruments over the years reflected in his style of indie pop, edm and dubstep. Before becoming eden he was in a group called the eden project this is where most of the dubstep and edm happened and due to him wanting to go into indie pop he left the group and became eden in 2015 in an effort to rebrand and do the music he is passionate about. Eden was born on the 23rd decemeber 1995 to a chinese father and an irish mother in the city of Dublin. Later on he taught himslef guitar, drums and piano. it then took till he was 18 to release his fist track under the Eden Project brand and releasing an albulm the following year. his first tour took place in the years of 2015 to 2016. the tour was known as end credits and saw him travelling the world the next tour was in early 2018 called vertigo vergo.
